Welcome to Wild Zim Heroes 🌿

In Zimbabwe, endangered species like the African wild dog and black rhino face extinction due to habitat loss, poaching, and human-wildlife conflict. Many young people aren’t aware of these threats—or how to help. Our gamified website, Wild Zim Heroes, solves this by letting users ‘adopt’ a virtual animal, complete real-world conservation tasks, and track their impact—turning awareness into action.

🐾 Virtual Adoption

Adopt endangered species like the Black Rhino, Pangolin, or Lion and give them a name and profile.

🌍 Real-Life Impact

Complete daily conservation tasks like reducing plastic use or sharing awareness photos.

🎮 Gamified Learning

Take fun quizzes and play interactive games that teach conservation principles and Zimbabwean wildlife facts.

📊 Progress Tracking

Track your animal’s "Habitat Health" and earn badges as you make progress.

🛡 Community Leaderboard

See how your efforts compare to other heroes on our national leaderboard.
